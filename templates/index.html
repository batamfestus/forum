Our Mission

At FOPGEMOV, we are dedicated to creating a world where gender equity, ethics, and moral values are upheld. Our focus is on promoting peaceful coexistence and proper sanitation and hygiene in schools and rural communities in Northeast Nigeria.

Through our programs and projects, we provide essential health care services, WASH initiatives, education, protection, and empowerment. We are committed to motivating positive trends towards knowledge and skill acquisition, driving positive change in the region.

Join us in our mission to create a better future for all.
